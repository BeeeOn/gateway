size_t DataWriter::write ( DataIterator &  iterator)

Writes all the data provided by the given DataIterator to the output stream.

Each data are written to the one line in the format: "HEXACHECKSUM""DATA"

  • "HEXACHECKSUM" is CRC-32 checksum of the following data in hexadecimal form
  • "DATA" is the exact data given by the DataIterator
