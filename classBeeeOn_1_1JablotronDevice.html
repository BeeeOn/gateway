int JablotronDevice::extractBatteryLevel ( const std::string &  battery)
protected

Extracts battery level from string. String contains two parts. The first part is "LB" (value type) and the second part is battery level. Delimiter of these two parts is colon. Battery level is 0 or 1. 0 doesn't indicate low battery level (100%), and 1 indicates low battery level (5%). Example of input string: LB:0 or LB:1

See: https://www.turris.cz/gadgets/manual
